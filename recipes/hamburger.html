Hamburger

Description

A hamburger can be created by collectiong the necessary resourcer from one's refridgerator or optionally by having a cow ready to be cooked.

Some would say that the perfect hamburger does not contain any parts of an animal. The opinion that I believe to be more widely accepted is that it is necessary for a hamburger to be made from freshly cooked beef. Another important ingredient of a hamburger is the sauce. Additionally one would add some lettuce, but one thing that can not be left out is the cheese.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • bread
  • sauce
  • cheese
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • cucumber

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cut the bread
  3. Cut some lettuce
  4. Cut some onion / tomato
  5. Make the sauce
  6. Put the ingredients together and enjoy the meal!
