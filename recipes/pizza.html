Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of the most known type of dishes available in most countires of the world. I believe that it is incredibly hard to find a child who would rather not eat pizza.

The history of this dish is somewhat interesting. My understanding is that it was first made as a last resource, because they used all the available ingredients in their home to make the first pizza. By today's standards I am confident that we would not even dare call that a pizza.

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • bread
  • beef/chicken or any meat
  • cucumber
  • sauce
  • lettuce
  • tomato

Steps

  1. Cut the meat
  2. Cut some lettuce
  3. Cut some onion / tomato
  4. Cut the bread
  5. Make the sauce
  6. Put the ingredients together, cook it and enjoy the meal!
