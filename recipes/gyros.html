Gyros

Description

A gyros is the national food of Greece. It is not really, but many believe so. It does in fact originate from there. It is delicious, it is cheap, it is semi-healthy.

The secret of the perfect gyros is to grab everything that you have at home and put it into a tortilla or a pita, or just simply use a plate. It is never a bad choice to have gyros for lunch.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • bread
  • sauce
  • tortilla
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • cucumber

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cut the bread
  3. Cut some lettuce
  4. Cut some onion / tomato
  5. Make the sauce
  6. Put the ingredients together and enjoy the meal!
